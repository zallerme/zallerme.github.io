What does it mean to be an "Alpha" Chapter?

The Alpha chapter is the first found chapter of a sorority or fraternity. It means that that organization was founded on that campus. Delta Zeta was founded on Miami University's campus on October 24th, 1902 by Alfa Lloyd, Mabelle Minton, Anne Simmons, Mary Collins, Anna Keen, and Julia Bishop. The national headquarters are located right here in Oxford.

"The women of Delta Zeta Sorority share a common purpose: to make life fuller, more meaningful, more rewarding."
